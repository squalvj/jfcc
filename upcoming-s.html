PANEL DISCUSSION

JFCC Panel Speaker Event : Minister Ignasius Jonan

Thursday, October 12 2017
4pm - 6pm
back to upcoming events

Dear JFCC members and friends,

Energy and Mineral Resources Minister, Ignasius Jonan, made headlines after Indonesia secured majority local ownership of the Grasberg gold and copper mine, often seen as the “crown jewel” of U.S. giant Freeport-McMoRan Inc.

The former banker and transport minister also earned a reputation of being tough after reforming the operations of state railway firm Kereta Api Indonesia.

Last October, Minister Jonan was tapped by President Joko Widodo to lead one of the most high-profile ministries in Indonesia, which has oversight over resources ranging from coal to copper. His appointment also came at a time when the rising energy needs in Southeast Asia’s biggest economy risk outpacing the development of oil and gas fields.

Please join the Jakarta Foreign Correspondents Club for a discussion with Minister Jonan.

Day / Date : Thursday, October 12 2017
Venue : Ayana Midplaza Hotel Jakarta
Time : 4pm – 6pm
RSVP : event@jfcc.info or call 021-2985-8007
Fee : IDR300.000 members (journalists)
IDR320.000 members (associates)
IDR350.000 members (walk-in)
IDR450.000 non-members
